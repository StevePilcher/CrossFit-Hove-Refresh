What is CrossFit?

CrossFit is a mixture of aerobic exercises, calisthenics and Olympic weightlifting. An hour-long class will include a warm-up, a skill development segment (typically Olympic liftiing) and finish with the high-intesity workout of the day (WOD).

Where to start

All new clients start their training with five one-on-one sessions. Each of the introduction sessions focuses on the movements we use and will incorporate a short workout which will focus on these movements.

Our Classes

Our classes are small and intimate where our CrossFit Certified Coaches not only know your name, but also know your capabilities, and will drive you further to reach new levels. We pride ourselves on our coaching abilities and the ability to get the best out of you.

The Workouts

The movements that we use are functional (replicated in life, requiring no special machinery or equipment). We dont look to train individual muscles in isolation but instead use compound (multiple joint) movements which mirror our movements outside of our training. Training is progressive and difficulty is increased as you grow in strength.
